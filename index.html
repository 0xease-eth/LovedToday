Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2026

Our Commitment

Loved Today is designed with privacy as its core principle. Your intimate life is personal, and we believe it should stay that way.

In short: We don't collect, store, or have access to any of your data. Period.

Data Storage

All your data is stored:

  • Locally on your device — Your check-in records are saved securely on your iPhone
  • In your personal iCloud — Data syncs securely across your own devices through your Apple ID, protected by Apple's end-to-end encryption

We do not operate any servers. Your data stays exclusively within your Apple ecosystem — only you can access it.

Apple Watch Support

You can record intimate activity using the Apple Watch's built-in "Cycle Tracking" app. Loved Today automatically syncs these records via HealthKit integration, so all your data appears in the iOS app.

Data We Don't Collect

  • Personal information
  • Usage analytics
  • Device identifiers
  • Location data
  • Any form of tracking

Apple Health Integration

If you choose to enable Health integration:

  • Data syncs directly between Loved Today and Apple Health on your device
  • This sync happens entirely on your device
  • We have no access to your Health data

Security

  • Face ID / Touch ID protection prevents unauthorized access
  • No widgets — your data never appears on your home screen
  • No account required — nothing to hack

Third Parties

We do not share data with third parties because we don't have your data to share.

Children's Privacy

Loved Today is intended for adults only (17+).

Changes to This Policy

If we update this policy, changes will be posted on this page.

FAQ

How do I back up my data?

Your data is automatically backed up through iCloud if enabled on your device. We recommend keeping iCloud backup enabled for your device.

No. Your data is tied to your Apple ID and can only be accessed on devices signed into your account.

I forgot to check in yesterday. Can I add it?

Yes! You can add records for past dates through the calendar view. Simply tap on any date in the calendar to add or remove records.

Is my data really private?

Yes. We have no servers, no accounts, no analytics. Your data exists only on your devices and in your personal iCloud storage.
